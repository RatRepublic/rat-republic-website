The Process

This tool utilizes your browser's local processing power to "grind" through millions of randomly generated Solana keypairs until it finds one that matches your desired prefix or suffix.

Local Computation

All cryptographic calculations are performed locally on your device using JavaScript.

Zero Data Transmission

Your CPU does the heavy lifting, but your sensitive data never leaves your window. No private keys, seeds, or generation patterns are ever sent to a server or stored in a database.

Deterministic Security

The randomness is generated using secure, browser-native cryptographic APIs to ensure your vanity address is as secure as one generated by a standard wallet.

Before You Generate

Please review these points before generating your address.

Finality of Keys

Once a vanity address is generated, you are responsible for safely backing up the private key or seed phrase. If you lose it, the address and any funds sent to it are permanently unrecoverable.

Resource Intensity

Generating long or complex vanity patterns requires significant CPU power. This may cause your browser to slow down or your device to heat up during the process.

Self-Custody Responsibility

This tool provides the keypair, but it does not store it. We cannot recover your keys if you close the tab before saving them.

Verify Environment

To ensure maximum security, we recommend running this tool in an Incognito/Private window and ensuring no malicious browser extensions are active while generating keys.
