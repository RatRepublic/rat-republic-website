  • Rent reclaim — closes empty token accounts to recover SOL

PRIVACY POLICY

Effective date: May 6, 2026

Short version: We collect only what's necessary to run the leaderboard and referral system. We never touch your private keys and we never sell your data.

When you register an account, we collect:

  • Email address — for account verification and recovery

  • Referral data — which referral link you signed up through, if any
  • Profile avatar — an image you upload voluntarily

When you use our tools without an account, we do not collect or store any personal information. Wallet connections and transactions happen entirely client-side through your browser wallet extension.

2. What We Never Collect

  • Private keys or seed phrases — we have no access to these at any time
  • Passwords in plain text — passwords are hashed before storage
  • Payment card or banking information

3. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Display your rank on the Crowned Vermins leaderboard
  • Track and distribute referral rewards

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the Service.

Our site uses browser localStorage to store:

This data lives only in your browser and is cleared when you log out.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your wallet address and username are visible on the public leaderboard if you choose to register — this is intentional and opt-in.

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us on X. On-chain transaction records cannot be deleted as they are part of the public blockchain.

We use industry-standard practices to protect stored data, including hashed passwords and token-based authentication. However, no system is 100% secure. Use a strong, unique password and enable two-factor authentication on your email account.
