Mode Guide
Even Split
Divide total equally across all wallets.
Proportional
Weight by each holder's token balance.
Custom
Fixed amount to every wallet in the list.
HOW TO USE
SOL
1
Paste the wallet addresses you want to send to — one address per line in the text box.
2
Choose a mode. Even Split divides a total SOL amount equally. Custom sends a fixed amount to every wallet.
3
Enter your SOL amount, then click Preview to review the breakdown before anything is sent.
4
Click Connect Wallet, approve in Phantom or Solflare, then click Send.
TOKENS
1
Paste the recipient wallet addresses — one per line.
2
Click Connect Wallet — your token balances will load into the dropdown automatically.
3
Select the token to send from the dropdown.
4
Choose Even Split or Custom, enter the amount, then click Preview.
5
Click Send. If a recipient doesn't have a token account yet, one will be created automatically (~0.00204 SOL rent per account, charged to you).
SOL & TOKENS
1
Enter the Holder Mint — the token mint address whose holders you want to target. Set the correct Decimals (usually 6 or 9).
2
Set a Min and/or Max balance to filter out small wallets or large holders. Leave Max blank to include everyone above the minimum.
3
Click Fetch Holders. The tool scans all token accounts on-chain and loads the qualifying list.
4
Paste any LP pool or vesting addresses into the Exclude field — one per line — to remove them from the airdrop.
5
Choose a distribution mode: Even Split (equal share), Proportional (weighted by holdings), or Custom (fixed amount per wallet).
6
For the Tokens tab — click Connect Wallet first so your token balances load into the dropdown, then select the token to send.
7
Click Preview to review recipient count, amounts, transactions needed, and service fee — then click Send.
