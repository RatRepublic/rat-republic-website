The Concept

On the Solana blockchain, every individual token account requires a small "rent" deposit of approximately 0.00203928 SOL to exist. If you've held tokens that were eventually sold, rugged, or migrated, those empty accounts are still holding your SOL hostage. This utility identifies those abandoned, zero-balance accounts and shuts them down, instantly returning the locked rent to your main wallet.

Security & Trust

Your assets remain under your control.

  • Manual Approval: Every action requires your direct signature via your wallet provider (like Phantom).
  • Zero-Balance Protection: The system only interacts with accounts that are completely empty.
  • Non-Custodial: No tokens are ever transferred out of your possession.
Does this impact my eligibility for airdrops?

Absolutely not. Airdrops are tied to your primary wallet address, not specific sub-accounts.

Why is my "claimable" balance showing as zero?

The tool specifically filters for accounts with a balance of exactly 0. If an account holds even 0.000001 of a token, it is considered active and will not be displayed.

Expert Mode — Full Toolkit

Expert mode unlocks every cleanup tool available. Each tab targets a different type of on-chain clutter.

CLAIM — Empty Account Closer

Scans your wallet for SPL token accounts with a balance of exactly 0 and closes them in batches. Each closed account returns ~0.00203928 SOL minus the service fee.

SCANNER — No-Connect Preview

Paste any Solana wallet address to instantly see how many empty accounts it holds — no wallet connection required.

TOKENS — Burn Fungible Tokens

Lists every SPL token in your wallet that still carries a balance. Selecting a token burns its entire supply and closes the account, returning the ~0.00203928 SOL rent. Use with care: the token balance is permanently destroyed.

NFT — Burn NFTs & pNFTs

Displays every NFT and programmable NFT in your wallet. Burning destroys the NFT on-chain permanently and reclaims the locked SOL.

By proceeding, you acknowledge that you are solely responsible for verifying the accounts you choose to close.

Irreversible Action

The process of reclaiming SOL by closing empty token accounts is permanent and non-reversible.

No Support Recovery

Because this is a decentralized on-chain action, no developer, wallet provider, or protocol can reverse this transaction.

By using Expert mode, you confirm that you understand the irreversible nature of these actions and accept full responsibility for every transaction you sign.

TOKENS — Burning with Balance

The TOKENS tab burns tokens that still hold a balance. This is not a transfer — the token supply is destroyed at the protocol level. No refund is possible.

NFT — Burning NFTs & pNFTs

Burning an NFT destroys the mint account, token account, and on-chain metadata permanently.

No Recovery — Ever

Rat Republic has no custody over your assets and cannot intervene once a transaction is signed and submitted.
