What Is Rat Republic?

Rat Republic is a non-custodial suite of Solana tools built for the community. Every operation requires your direct signature through Phantom or Solflare — we never have custody of your assets.

Tools are designed to save time, recover locked SOL, and give you full control over your on-chain footprint. A small service fee per operation sustains the platform.

Scavenger

Close abandoned zero-balance token accounts and reclaim their locked SOL rent (~0.00204 SOL per account).

How It Works

On Solana, every SPL token account requires a rent deposit of approximately 0.00203928 SOL to exist. When you sell, transfer, or hold tokens that were rugged or abandoned, those empty accounts remain open — trapping your SOL indefinitely.

Scavenger identifies all zero-balance token accounts in your wallet and closes them in batches of up to 27 per transaction, returning the locked rent directly to you minus a small service fee.

How to Use

1
Go to the Scavenger page
Navigate to ratrepublic.art/cleanup. You'll land in NOOB mode by default.
2
Connect your wallet
Click "Connect Wallet". Select Phantom or Solflare from the modal.
3
Let it scan
Scavenger automatically scans all your token accounts and displays the ones that are safe to close with their estimated reclaim amount.
4
Select and claim
All eligible accounts are pre-selected. Deselect any you want to keep, then click "Claim SOL".
5
Approve and done
Approve the transaction in your wallet. SOL arrives in seconds. A Solscan link appears for each batch.

Save on fees: Register via a referral link to unlock the 1.8% rate — a 10% discount from the standard 2.0%. Registration alone (without being referred) does not reduce the fee.

Safety

  • Manual Approval — Every action requires your direct signature.
  • Zero-Balance Only — The CLAIM tab only closes accounts with a balance of exactly 0. Accounts with any remaining token balance are never touched.
  • Non-Custodial — No assets are transferred out of your control.
  • Airdrop Eligibility — Closing empty accounts does not affect your eligibility for future airdrops. Airdrops target your primary wallet address.

Note: Closing accounts is permanent. However, a new token account is automatically created when someone sends that token to you again (costs ~0.00204 SOL to reopen).

Pro Tips

  • If an account holds even 0.000001 of a token it won't appear in CLAIM — use the TOKENS tab in Expert Mode to burn it.
  • The SCANNER tab lets you check any wallet address without connecting — useful for helping friends or checking your cold wallet.

Vanity Address

Generate a custom Solana wallet address that starts or ends with any text of your choice — entirely in your browser, for free.

What Is a Vanity Address?

A vanity address is a standard Solana wallet where the public key begins or ends with a specific string you chose — for example, a wallet that starts with RATxxx.... The wallet is cryptographically identical to any other Solana wallet and fully functional.

The tool works by generating thousands of keypairs per second using a background Web Worker until one matches your pattern.

How to Use

1
Enter your prefix or suffix
Type the string you want your address to start or end with. Solana addresses are Base58 — no 0, O, I, or l characters.
2
Click Generate
The generator runs entirely in your browser using a Web Worker thread. No data is sent to any server at any point.
3
Copy and save your keypair
When a match is found, copy both the public and private keys to a secure location. Import the private key into Phantom or any Solana wallet that accepts raw private keys.

Security

Your private key never leaves your device. All generation runs locally in a browser Web Worker. Rat Republic never sees your private key, seed phrase, or any generated keypair.

  • Zero network requests are made during key generation
  • Store your private key in a password manager or hardware wallet immediately after generation
  • Never share your private key with anyone or any website
  • A vanity address has the same security properties as any other randomly generated Solana keypair

Performance

Longer patterns take exponentially more time to find:

Pattern LengthAverage Time
1 characterInstant
2 charactersA few seconds
3 charactersSeconds to minutes
4 charactersMinutes
5+ charactersHours or longer

Speed depends on your CPU. The generator displays your current generation rate in real time. Desktop computers are significantly faster than mobile devices.

Fee

Vanity Address generation is FREE — no wallet connection required.

Token Burn

Permanently destroy unwanted SPL tokens (including Token 2022) and reclaim the rent deposit locked in the token account (~0.00204 SOL).

Expert Mode only. Token Burn is in the TOKENS tab of the Scavenger page. Enable Expert Mode at the top of the page to access it.

Why Burn Instead of Sending to a Burn Address?

Burning via a native Solana instruction is strictly better than sending tokens to a dead address like 1nc1nerator...:

  • You get your SOL back — burning closes the token account and returns ~0.00204 SOL rent to you. Sending to an incinerator leaves that SOL locked in the dead account forever.
  • Tokens are actually destroyed — the total supply decreases on-chain, visible on any explorer. Tokens at an incinerator address still technically exist and count toward supply.
  • Cleaner wallet — the token account is fully closed, not just emptied. Your wallet shows fewer clutter accounts.
  • Deflationary pressure — reducing circulating supply can positively affect a token's price and metrics.

Safety: Can Airdropped Tokens Drain You?

Burning a token account uses a standard native Solana instruction (BurnChecked + CloseAccount). This cannot execute any custom program logic — it's handled entirely by the SPL Token Program, not any token creator's contract.

What to watch for: Token drains happen through approval-based attacks — when you sign a transaction that grants a program delegation over your tokens. The Rat Republic Token Burn tool only uses native burn and close instructions and never requests any token delegation or approval.

FAQ

Answers to the most common questions.

Scavenger

Does closing empty accounts affect my airdrop eligibility?

No. Airdrops are distributed to your primary wallet address. Token sub-accounts are just storage containers — closing them has no effect on your eligibility for any project's airdrop.

Why is my claimable balance showing zero?

Scavenger's CLAIM tab only shows accounts with a balance of exactly 0. If an account holds even 0.000001 of a token it is considered active. Use the TOKENS tab in Expert Mode to burn those accounts.

Can I undo a closed account?

No, closing is permanent. However, if you need to receive that token again in the future, a new token account is automatically created when someone sends it to your wallet (at a cost of ~0.00204 SOL).

Is it safe to close airdropped token accounts?

The CLAIM tab only closes accounts with zero balance — it never touches tokens you hold. For token accounts with balance (including suspicious airdrops), the TOKENS tab lets you review and burn them with full confirmation. Native burn instructions cannot execute custom contract code.

Vanity Address

Does Rat Republic store my generated private key?

No. Generation runs entirely in your browser using a Web Worker. No keypair data is ever sent to a server.

Is a vanity address as secure as a regular address?

Yes. The cryptographic security of a Solana keypair is determined by the entropy used to generate it — not by what the public key looks like. A vanity address is as secure as any other properly generated keypair.

What Base58 characters are valid?

Valid characters: 123456789ABCDEFGHJKLMNPQRSTUVWXYZabcdefghijkmnopqrstuvwxyz. The four excluded characters are 0 (zero), O (capital o), I (capital i), and l (lowercase L) — they're removed to avoid visual confusion.

Why is generation slow on my phone?

Mobile CPUs are significantly slower than desktop CPUs for this type of computation. For anything longer than 3 characters, a desktop or laptop is recommended.

PFP Generator

What is the PFP Generator?

The PFP Generator lets you create a custom Rat Republic-themed avatar by mixing and matching trait layers. Download your creation and use it as your profile picture anywhere.

Are generated images stored on Rat Republic servers?

No. Images are composed and downloaded on your device. Nothing is uploaded to Rat Republic servers.

Token & NFT Burn

What happens to burned tokens on-chain?

The token balance is permanently reduced to zero and the account is closed. The token mint's total supply decreases by the burned amount, visible on any Solana explorer. This is a native Solana instruction — there is no way to reverse it.

Can I accidentally burn a valuable token?

The tool displays the token name, symbol, balance, and image (from Metaplex metadata) before you select anything. A separate confirmation modal requires an explicit acknowledgment before the transaction is signed. Always review carefully.

Why do NFTs recover significantly more SOL than regular tokens?

NFTs have two additional on-chain accounts that regular tokens don't: a Metaplex metadata account (~0.012 SOL, stores name/symbol/URI/creators) and a Master Edition account (~0.003 SOL, stores edition and supply data). Burning an NFT closes all three accounts and returns the full ~0.017 SOL.

Does burning an NFT remove it from marketplaces?

Yes. Once burned, the NFT's on-chain data is destroyed. It will no longer appear in your wallet, on marketplaces, or in any explorer. Listings may need time to update, but the NFT itself is gone permanently.
